Since we use these services, they may collect your data if you allow it in your Dash! Ball application settings (iOS & Android).
For Firebase Analytics we don’t collect any personal information and ID, all data is anonymous. 
These Firebase Analytics data is used for better experience and don’t have any information about user’s personal identity. 
All Firebase Analytics data is optional, and you may opt out from your Dash! Ball application settings from phone settings. You may also try Dash! Ball offline.
Other than these services we don’t use any services that collect your information.
We do not sell, rent or trade any of your personal data.
