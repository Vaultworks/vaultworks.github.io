Since we use these services, they may collect your data (IDFA for iOS & GAID for Android devices) and if you allow it in your Dash! Ball application settings (iOS & Android).
For Firebase Analytics & Facebook, Dash! Ball doesn't collect any personal information and ID, all data is anonymous at our end.
These Firebase Analytics data are used for a better experience and don’t have any information about the user’s identity. 
All Firebase Analytics data is optional, and you may opt-out from your Dash! Ball application settings from phone settings. You may also try Dash! Ball offline.
Other than these services we don’t use any services that collect your information.
We do not sell, rent or trade any of your data.
For further information & to submit all your right claims, you can e-mail us from support@vaultworks.studio or fill the form from https://bit.ly/VaultworksContact anytime! Your requests will be answered as soon as possible and within thirty days at the latest.
